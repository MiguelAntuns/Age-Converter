The formula to calculate (Read Article) and convert dog age to human age is quite simple. The first year of a dog's life is roughly the equivalent to 15 human years. The second year of a dog's life is equal to about 9 human years, and then the following years equal to about 4 or 5 human years. The formula can differentiate depending on the dogs size.
